What We Do

Our Services

From small repairs to full room replasters — quality plastering for homes across Norfolk.

Skim Plastering

Fresh skim coat applied to walls and ceilings for a smooth, professional finish ready for decorating. No bumps, no imperfections.

Crack & Ceiling Repairs

Cracks in your walls or ceilings sorted properly — not just filled and painted over. Long-lasting repairs you can't see.

Artex & Textured Ceilings

Cover over old artex without the mess of removal. Lewis uses a bonding approach that leaves your ceilings looking brand new.

Full Room Replaster

Complete replastering of rooms from scratch — walls, ceilings, or both. Ideal for renovations, extensions, and new builds.

Fireplace & Feature Plastering

Specialist plasterwork around fireplaces, alcoves, and feature walls — finished to the highest standard.

About Us

Norwich's Trusted Plasterer

L.S. Plastering Services is run by Lewis, a Norwich-based plasterer trained and taught by the best in the trade. With a reputation built entirely on quality and word of mouth, Lewis takes real pride in delivering a flawless finish on every job.

You'll always deal directly with Lewis — from the first call to the final tidy-up. Punctual, professional, and genuinely passionate about the craft.
